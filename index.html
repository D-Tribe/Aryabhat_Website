Frequently Asked Questions

Does Aryabhat support third-party cloud storage ?
Yes. Aryabhat supports integration with leading third-party cloud storage services, including OneDrive, Google Drive, and Amazon S3 buckets. This enables users to securely upload and manage personal or organizational documents.
Does Aryabhat retain conversation history ?
Yes. Aryabhat is context-aware and retains conversation history, allowing users to ask follow-up questions and continue discussions without needing to restate prior information. This ensures a more seamless and efficient user experience compared to traditional search tools.
How does Aryabhat handle multi-language environments ?
Not at all. Aryabhat is designed for non-technical users. Employees can ask questions in plain language—by text or voice—and Aryabhat automatically translates them into secure database queries and document searches behind the scenes.
How are user licenses and access managed ?
User licenses and access are fully managed by administrators. Aryabhat provides comprehensive usage analytics and dashboard insights, allowing administrators to monitor adoption, track system usage, and maintain governance across the organization.
